Nuxeo Platform has a distributed architecture. It is compatible with a wide range of backends. Hence there is many options for addressing your scalability requirements. From simple and small deployments to billion records repositories, how Nuxeo Platform behaves? Awesome cloud automation helps us answering that question: Nuxeo Platform rocks! Just have a tour on our per-release benchmarks, continuous ones and extra-special ones. Watch figures, compare, learn the architectures, and give feedback!

If you had only those figures to remember...

1,000,000,000
That's the maximum number of documents stored in a Nuxeo Platform instance. Read the details on the Nuxeo Blog and stay tuned, we now are targetting ten billions!
6000 req/s
On a business scenario mix read and write calls, benchmarking a JavaScript front application hitting Nuxeo Platform server, we reach 6000 requests per seconds. Read the details on the Nuxeo Blog.
14000 documents/sec
That's the import speed when using a MongoDB backend, which makes this backend an ideal one for projects with huge batches of data to import. Read more on the Nuxeo Blog.
